Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 13, 2026

Overview

Number Crush is an offline number puzzle game. We do not require an account, and we do not collect personal information from players.

Information We Collect

Number Crush does not collect, transmit, sell, or share personal information. Game progress, settings, and best scores may be stored locally on your device so the app can remember your preferences and results.

Network Access

The app is designed to work offline. It does not use third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, social login, or external tracking services.

Children's Privacy

Number Crush does not knowingly collect personal information from children or any other users. Because the app does not collect personal information, there is no personal data to request, delete, or export.

Data Sharing

We do not share personal information with third parties. Locally stored game data remains on your device unless you delete the app or your device operating system backs it up according to your own device settings.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy if the app changes. The effective date at the top of this page will be updated when changes are made.

Contact

For privacy questions about Number Crush, contact the developer through the support channel listed on the App Store product page.
